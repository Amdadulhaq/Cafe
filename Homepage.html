Welcome to Detroit City FC

Welcome to the home of Detroit City FC, where passion meets pride on the soccer field. Established with a commitment to delivering thrilling and competitive soccer matches,
Detroit City FC stands as a symbol of the vibrant spirit of our city. Our club is more than just a team; it's a community united by the love of the game and a shared sense of belonging.
Join us on this exhilarating journey as we celebrate the beautiful game, forge lasting connections, and proudly represent Detroit. At Detroit City FC, it's not just about the goals on the field;
it's about the shared moments, the unwavering support, and the indomitable spirit of our dedicated fans. Experience the excitement, feel the camaraderie, and become part of the Detroit City FC family.
